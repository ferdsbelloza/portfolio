Hello it's Jenna

I'm a Professional dancer


Since I was young, I've dreamt of being on stage, the spotlight cascading on me as I play different roles. Over the years, I've been a full-time ballerina working in London, St. Petersburg, Shanghai and Hong Kong. Now based in Hong Kong, I'm taking inspiration from the ballet movements when East meets West.

After studying ballet since childhood and entering full-time training in London, Jenna later decided that contemporary dance offered a broader range of pursuits and expressions important to her development taking her to The School of Contemporary Dance. As her field of vision and experience expanded, she was enticed to Moscow where she spent two years exploring the realms of physical performance while investigating and developing new concepts and ideas. Jenna works on a project basis with choreographers, artists, production houses and creative collaborators in Russia, London, Hong Kong and the US.
